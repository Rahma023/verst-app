Certificate PDFs, role-aware UX, Program-at-a-glance panel, 80% pass mark

Certificates: @react-pdf/renderer-based VerstCertificate component
(forest-green branded A4 landscape with learner name, module, date,
verify code). /api/certificate/[id]/pdf streams the PDF after RLS check.
/verify/[code] is a public page using a new SECURITY DEFINER RPC
(get_cert_by_verify_code in schema-004) so anonymous visitors can
verify without leaking user_id. Dashboard adds a 'Your certificates'
section with Download PDF + Verify link buttons.

Role-aware UX: getUserAndRole helper centralises role lookup. TopNav
passes role + portalHref to AuthControls; the avatar links to /admin /
/tutor / /dashboard based on role and shows the role under the first
name. SigninModal and SignupModal redirect to the role-specific portal
on success. /dashboard redirects non-learners to their portal. On
/program/[id] the Enrol button is replaced with 'Manage in Tutor
Portal' (for assigned tutors), 'Manage in Studio' (for admins), or a
small 'You don't teach this module' note (for unassigned tutors).

Quiz: pass mark raised from 60% to 80%.

Program page polish: hero is now 2-column. Right column is a
'Program at a glance' panel — section-A/B count tiles, stats list
(modules, lessons, hours, levels, pass mark), tape-note about the
intended audience.
